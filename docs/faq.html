Getting Started

New to geo-grid rank tracking? Start here to understand the basics of how GeoHeatWhisper works.

What is a geo-grid heatmap?

A geo-grid breaks down a geographic area (city, neighborhood, zip code) into a grid of cells. GeoHeatWhisper checks your Google Maps ranking from each cell's location, creating a visual heatmap that shows:

  • Red (Rank 1–3) where you rank at the very top
  • Orange (Rank 4–10) where you have good visibility
  • Yellow (Rank 11–20) where you have lower visibility
  • Grey (Unranked) where you don't appear in results

This reveals the spatial reality of local SEO: you might rank #1 downtown but #15 in the suburbs.

How is this different from regular rank tracking?

Traditional rank checkers only show your position from a single point. GeoHeatWhisper reveals how your rankings vary across an entire area. This is critical because:

  • Google shows different results based on searcher location
  • Proximity to your business affects rankings
  • Competitors may dominate certain neighborhoods while you dominate others
  • Visual heatmaps make it easy to identify gaps and opportunities

Do I need technical SEO knowledge to use GeoHeatWhisper?

No! GeoHeatWhisper is designed for anyone who needs to track local rankings, even without SEO expertise. The visual heatmaps are intuitive — red means you're ranking well, grey means you're not appearing.

What type of businesses benefit most from geo-grid tracking?

Any business with a physical location or service area benefits, especially:

  • Multi-location businesses (franchises, retail chains, healthcare networks)
  • Service providers with defined territories (plumbers, electricians, landscapers)
  • Local SEO agencies managing client portfolios
  • Businesses competing in crowded markets where visibility varies by neighborhood

Credits & Grid Sizes

Understanding how credits work and managing your monthly allocation.

How do credits work?

Each grid point uses one credit. The number of credits per scan depends on grid size:

  • 1×1 grid: 1 credit (single-point scan, available on Free)
  • 3×3 grid: 9 credits
  • 5×5 grid: 25 credits
  • 7×7 grid: 49 credits

You can choose grid size when creating a search to balance detail vs. credit consumption.

How many credits do I get per month?

  • Free Plan: 10 credits/month (enough for one 3×3 scan plus a single-point scan)
  • Pro Plan ($49/mo): 1,000 credits/month (up to 20 large 7×7 scans)
  • Agency Plan ($249/mo): Unlimited credits

What happens if I exceed my credit limit?

On Free and Pro plans, you cannot create new searches once you've used your monthly credits. Your existing searches and historical data remain accessible. Your credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. You can upgrade anytime for more credits.

Do credits roll over?

No. Credits reset each month and unused credits do not carry over to the next billing period.

What grid sizes are available on the Free plan?

The Free plan only supports 1×1 single-point scans (1 credit each). Grid sizes 3×3, 5×5, and 7×7 require a Pro or Agency plan.

Keywords & Tracking

Managing keywords and understanding tracking capabilities.

What types of keywords should I track?

Focus on keywords that match your services or products with local intent:

  • Service keywords: "plumber", "dentist", "personal injury lawyer"
  • Near me variations: "pizza near me", "urgent care near me"
  • Location modifiers: "plumber in [city]", "[neighborhood] restaurant"
  • Long-tail variations: "emergency AC repair", "family dentist"

Can I track competitors' rankings?

Yes! On Pro and Agency plans, you can track up to 5 competitors per market. Use "Compare vs. My Business" to see side-by-side heatmaps showing exactly where competitors outrank you.

How many rankings are shown per grid point?

Each grid point shows the top 20 Google Maps results for your keyword from that location. You can see your exact position and all competing businesses.

Technical Questions

Technical details about how GeoHeatWhisper works.

Where does ranking data come from?

We query Google Maps directly from each grid cell location, reflecting what real users see when searching from those locations.

How accurate is the data?

Our data reflects actual Google Maps results from each location. However, rankings can vary slightly due to:

  • Google's personalization algorithms
  • Time of day and search history
  • Device type and browser

We provide the most accurate snapshot possible at the time of the search.

Does GeoHeatWhisper work internationally?

Yes! GeoHeatWhisper works in any country where Google Maps operates. Simply enter the location in the local format (address, city, postal code).

Why do some grid points appear in water?

GeoHeatWhisper simulates Google's ranking algorithm, which includes proximity searches from mobile users near coastlines, bridges, and waterfronts. These water points still return valid ranking data because Google serves results to users in those areas. Learn more

Why might keyword ranking differ from business ranking?

Keyword rankings reflect how well a business matches search intent. Business rankings reflect overall authority compared to similar businesses. A business can rank #1 for a keyword but lower for its own name. Learn more
