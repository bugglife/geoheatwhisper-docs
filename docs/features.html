GeoHeatWhisper Features

Complete breakdown of all capabilities for local SEO rank tracking, heatmap visualization, and competitive analysis.

Core Features (All Plans)

Geo-Grid Heatmaps

Visualize Google Maps rankings across geographic grids rather than single points. See exactly where you rank well and where you need improvement with color-coded heatmaps.

  • Interactive grid cells showing position-by-position rankings
  • Grid sizes: 1×1 (Free), 3×3, 5×5, 7×7 (Pro/Agency)
  • Color coding: Red (Rank 1–3), Orange (Rank 4–10), Yellow (Rank 11–20), Grey (Unranked)
  • Click-through to see competing businesses in each cell
  • View top 20 rankings per grid point

Neighborhood Tracking

Track rankings block-by-block within your target market. Understand how your visibility changes across different neighborhoods, zip codes, and districts.

  • Drill down from city-wide to neighborhood-specific grids
  • Compare performance across multiple neighborhoods
  • Identify underserved areas for geographic expansion
  • Track proximity effects on ranking distribution

Basic Map View

All plans include an interactive map view showing your geo-grid results overlaid on a real map, making it easy to understand rankings in geographic context.

Pro Features ($49/mo or $349/yr)

Competitor Tracking Pro Agency

Track up to 5 competitors per market. See how your rankings compare against competitors across the same geo-grid.

  • Compare vs. My Business — side-by-side heatmap comparisons
  • "Scan from here" — run a scan from any grid point
  • Identify areas where competitors outrank you
  • Track competitive shifts over time

Automated Email Reports Pro Agency

Receive automated ranking reports delivered to your inbox on a weekly or monthly schedule.

  • Weekly or monthly email delivery
  • Ranking change summaries
  • Competitor position updates

Historical Data & Trends Pro Agency

Track ranking changes over time with up to 12 months of historical data. Measure campaign effectiveness and identify trends.

  • Rank history charts showing trends by location
  • Snapshot comparison — compare heatmaps from different dates
  • Before/after campaign analysis
  • Identify seasonal patterns

Save to Leads Pro Agency

Save businesses from search results to your leads list for prospecting and outreach.

  • Save business names, addresses, and phone numbers
  • Add tags and notes for organization
  • CSV export for CRM integration

Advanced Analytics Pro Agency

Comprehensive performance metrics beyond basic ranking data.

  • Average ranking position across grids
  • Coverage percentage (% of grid cells where you appear)
  • Ranking distribution charts
  • Weakest coverage zones (Pro blurred preview, full on Agency)
  • Revenue impact estimates and competitive threat insights
